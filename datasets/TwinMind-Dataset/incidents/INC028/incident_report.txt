1. SUMMARY
========================================================================

Prometheus production instance (prometheus-prod-01) silently stopped scraping 23 critical targets at 09:30 UTC on July 21, 2025. A Prometheus configuration reload triggered by a service discovery update introduced a malformed scrape config that caused the scrape manager to skip an entire target group. The monitoring team was unaware of the blind spot for 4 hours until an unrelated investigation revealed missing metrics.

This incident was classified as P3 severity and was assigned to
Nina Patel in the Observability department. The affected system was
Prometheus Monitoring Stack, specifically the prometheus-prod-01 component located in US-East-1 (AWS).
The incident was detected through automated monitoring and alerting systems,
and the response team was engaged within minutes of the initial alert.

========================================================================
2. BUSINESS IMPACT
========================================================================

23 production services were unmonitored for 4 hours. During this blind spot, a minor performance degradation in the user-auth service went undetected. No alerting was possible for affected services. Grafana dashboards showed gaps in metric data. Team confidence in monitoring reliability was impacted.

The incident triggered the organization's major incident response process,

8. PREVENTIVE ACTIONS
========================================================================

The following preventive actions have been identified to reduce the
likelihood and impact of similar incidents in the future:

  1. Implement Prometheus configuration validation in CI before deployment
  2. Add meta-monitoring to alert when scrape targets go down
  3. Create canary scrape target that alerts if Prometheus stops collecting
  4. Implement scrape target count monitoring with change detection
  5. Add Prometheus config reload verification step with target validation

These action items will be tracked in the engineering backlog and prioritized
based on risk assessment and business impact analysis.
