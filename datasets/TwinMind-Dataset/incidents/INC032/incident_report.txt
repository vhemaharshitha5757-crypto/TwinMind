1. SUMMARY
========================================================================

The overnight production database backup job failed at 02:00 UTC on August 18, 2025. The backup service account password had expired after 90 days per the organization's password rotation policy, but the backup job configuration was not updated with the new credentials. The failure was not detected until the morning backup verification check at 08:00 UTC, meaning the daily backup was missed.

This incident was classified as P2 severity and was assigned to
Chris Evans in the Storage Engineering department. The affected system was
AWS Backup Service, specifically the backup-vault-prod-west component located in US-West-2 (AWS).
The incident was detected through automated monitoring and alerting systems,
and the response team was engaged within minutes of the initial alert.

========================================================================
2. BUSINESS IMPACT
========================================================================

Daily production database backup missed for one day. Recovery Point Objective (RPO) extended from 24 hours to 48 hours. Compliance audit finding generated for missed backup window. No data loss occurred but disaster recovery posture was weakened.

The incident triggered the organization's major incident response process,
involving cross-functional coordination between engineering, operations, and
business stakeholders. Customer support was notified and prepared to handle
incoming queries related to the service degradation.

========================================================================
8. PREVENTIVE ACTIONS
========================================================================

The following preventive actions have been identified to reduce the
likelihood and impact of similar incidents in the future:

  1. Implement service account credential rotation automation
  2. Add backup job success/failure monitoring with immediate alerts
  3. Use IAM roles instead of service account credentials where possible
  4. Create service account credential expiry dashboard
  5. Implement backup verification automation with immediate escalation on failure

These action items will be tracked in the engineering backlog and prioritized
based on risk assessment and business impact analysis.
